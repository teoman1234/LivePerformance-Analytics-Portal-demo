# SuperStar Influencer Consulting & Marketing Portal

## Product Overview

SuperStar Influencer Consulting & Marketing Portal is a SaaS platform built for TikTok-focused creator agencies. It provides:

- Supervisor dashboards for agency management
- Performance analytics for mentors / reps
- (Planned) self-service portal for influencers

The goal is to turn SuperStar Agency from a "classic" agency into a technology / digital service exporter in line with the Turkish Ministry of Trade incentives.

## Main Modules

- **Supervisor Dashboard** (already implemented)
  - Aggregated KPIs (tokens, live hours, followers, likes)
  - Proprietary scores: ABPS, TIS, COS
  - Group / mentor performance tables
  - Time filters: Today / This Week / Last 30 Days

- **Influencer Portal** (planned)
  - Creator-facing performance summary
  - Simplified scores and actionable insights
  - Communication channel between agency and influencers

## Target Users

- Agency owners / supervisors
- Team leaders / mentors
- TikTok influencers under the agency

## Tech Stack (High Level)

- Backend: FastAPI, SQLite
- Frontend: React (Vite), Chart.js
- Deployment target: Web (desktop-first dashboard)
